About Split Bill & Tip Calculator


This split bill and tip calculator works out tax, tip, total cost and the amount each person should pay, all in one simple result.


It is useful for restaurant bills, group meals, travel costs and other shared expenses where you want a quick per-person number without doing the math by hand.
