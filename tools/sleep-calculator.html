About Sleep Calculator


This sleep calculator suggests bedtimes or wake-up times based on 90-minute sleep cycles and a typical time to fall asleep.


It is useful for bedtime planning, early-morning schedules and adjusting routines when you want a more practical target than a random alarm time.
