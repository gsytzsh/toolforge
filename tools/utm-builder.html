About UTM Builder

This UTM builder creates campaign tracking URLs with standard UTM parameters such as source, medium and campaign.

It is useful for newsletters, paid ads, social posts and partner links when you want cleaner attribution data in analytics tools.

How to Use UTM Builder

  1. Step 1: Enter your website URL or meta information.
  2. Step 2: Configure the SEO parameters you want to generate.
  3. Step 3: Click Generate to create the tags or code.
  4. Step 4: Copy the output and add it to your webpage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know HTML?
No. Our tools generate the code for you. Just fill in your information and copy the output.
Where do I add the generated code?
Meta tags go in the <head> section of your HTML. Schema markup can be added as JSON-LD script anywhere on the page.
Will this improve my SEO?
Proper meta tags and structured data help search engines understand your content better, which can improve visibility in search results.
