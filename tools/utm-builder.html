About UTM Builder

This UTM builder creates campaign tracking URLs with standard UTM parameters such as source, medium and campaign.

It is useful for newsletters, paid ads, social posts and partner links when you want cleaner attribution data in analytics tools.

How to Use UTM Builder

  1. Step 1: enter base URL and campaign parameters
  2. Step 2: Fill source, medium and campaign consistently to keep reports clean.
  3. Step 3: Generate tracking URL with encoded parameters.
  4. Step 4: Copy campaign link into ads, emails or social posts.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is UTM Builder mainly used for?
UTM Builder is mainly used to build clean UTM campaign URLs for attribution and analytics reporting.
Does UTM Builder send my input to a server?
For normal usage, UTM Builder processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
Why do inconsistent UTM names hurt reporting?
Small naming differences split data into multiple buckets. Use a strict naming convention across teams.
