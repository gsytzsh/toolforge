About Text to PDF

This Text to PDF tool turns plain text into a simple paginated PDF. It is useful for notes, logs, snippets, drafts and quick printable text documents.

The conversion is done entirely in your browser, so your text stays on your device during export.

How to Use Text to PDF

  1. Step 1: paste plain text content to export
  2. Step 2: Adjust spacing and line breaks for readability.
  3. Step 3: Generate PDF from text input.
  4. Step 4: Download PDF for printing or sharing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Text to PDF mainly used for?
Text to PDF is mainly used to convert plain text into clean PDF documents quickly.
Does Text to PDF send my input to a server?
For normal usage, Text to PDF processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
How can I keep text wrapping readable in Text to PDF?
Use shorter line lengths and clear paragraph breaks before exporting to improve PDF readability.
