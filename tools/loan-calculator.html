About Loan Calculator

This loan calculator estimates the monthly payment and total repayment for a fixed-rate loan based on principal, rate and term.

It is useful for mortgages, car loans and personal loans when you want to compare borrowing options before applying or refinancing.

How to Use Loan Calculator

  1. Step 1: enter loan amount, interest rate and repayment period
  2. Step 2: Set term units and payment assumptions to match your contract.
  3. Step 3: Calculate installment amount and totals.
  4. Step 4: Use results to compare financing options or repayment plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Loan Calculator mainly used for?
Loan Calculator is mainly used to estimate monthly payments and total loan cost from principal, rate and term.
Does Loan Calculator send my input to a server?
For normal usage, Loan Calculator processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
Do loan calculator results include taxes and fees?
Most basic loan calculations focus on principal and interest only. Add fees, insurance and taxes separately.
