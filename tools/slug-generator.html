About Slug Generator

The Slug Generator tool lets you turn a title into a clean, URL-friendly slug for blog posts, pages and products.

It is useful for editing copy, cleaning pasted text, comparing content and making small text transformations directly in the browser.

How to Use Slug Generator

  1. Step 1: enter the title or phrase to transform
  2. Step 2: Choose separator and casing style for your URL convention.
  3. Step 3: Generate slug output.
  4. Step 4: Use the slug in route paths, filenames or CMS URLs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Slug Generator mainly used for?
Slug Generator is mainly used to convert text titles into URL-friendly slugs for pages and posts.
Does Slug Generator send my input to a server?
For normal usage, Slug Generator processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
Should slugs include stop words like 'the' and 'of'?
They can, but concise slugs are usually cleaner. Keep URLs readable and stable rather than over-optimizing.
