About Screenshot to PDF

This Screenshot to PDF tool combines screenshots into a single PDF for sharing walkthroughs, bug reports, UI reviews and visual notes. Each screenshot is scaled to fit a PDF page.

Everything is processed directly in your browser without uploading screenshots to a server.
