About Robots.txt Generator

This robots.txt generator helps you build a valid robots.txt file with common directives such as User-agent, Allow, Disallow and Sitemap.

It is useful when launching a site, blocking low-value paths, guiding search crawlers and making sure important pages are discoverable without exposing sections you do not want indexed.
