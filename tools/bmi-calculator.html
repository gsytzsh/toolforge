About BMI Calculator

This BMI calculator uses height and weight to estimate Body Mass Index and show the standard BMI category.

It is useful for quick wellness checks, fitness tracking and routine health planning when you want a simple body-weight reference point.

How to Use BMI Calculator

  1. Step 1: enter your height and weight values
  2. Step 2: Select metric or imperial units before calculating.
  3. Step 3: Calculate BMI score and category.
  4. Step 4: Review the range as a quick screening reference, not a medical diagnosis.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI Calculator mainly used for?
BMI Calculator is mainly used to calculate body mass index from height and weight inputs.
Does BMI Calculator send my input to a server?
For normal usage, BMI Calculator processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
Is BMI enough to evaluate overall health?
BMI is a useful indicator but not a full health assessment. Body composition and medical context also matter.
