About Permutation & Combination

This permutation and combination calculator gives you `nPr` and `nCr` from values of `n` and `r`.

It is useful for probability, counting problems, exam prep and planning scenarios where order matters in one case and not in the other.

How to Use Permutation Combination

  1. Step 1: Open Permutation Combination and enter your source input data in the main field.
  2. Step 2: Fill all required fields and confirm unit assumptions before calculation.
  3. Step 3: Run the calculation and review intermediate values where available.
  4. Step 4: Use the result in planning, estimation or comparison decisions.
