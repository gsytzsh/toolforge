About GPA Calculator

This GPA calculator lets you enter grades and course credits to estimate your grade point average.

It is useful for semester planning, academic tracking and checking how individual classes affect your overall GPA.

How to Use GPA Calculator

  1. Step 1: Open GPA Calculator and enter your source input data in the main field.
  2. Step 2: Fill all required fields and confirm unit assumptions before calculation.
  3. Step 3: Run the calculation and review intermediate values where available.
  4. Step 4: Use the result in planning, estimation or comparison decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GPA Calculator mainly used for?
GPA Calculator is mainly used to enter grades (A=4, B=3, C=2, D=1, F=0) and credits per course, one per line: e.g. A 3.
Does GPA Calculator send my input to a server?
For normal usage, GPA Calculator processes data locally in your browser so you can work without uploading project files.
Why do calculator results differ from another source?
Differences usually come from rounding precision, unit assumptions or formula variants used by each tool.
