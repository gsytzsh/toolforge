About Color Midpoint Calculator

This tool calculates the interpolated color between two hex colors at a given ratio. Ratio 0.5 gives the exact midpoint.

Useful for gradients, color ramps and design systems.

How to Use Color Midpoint Calculator

  1. Step 1: Upload your image or enter the color value.
  2. Step 2: Adjust the settings (size, format, compression, etc.).
  3. Step 3: Click Process to see the preview.
  4. Step 4: Download the processed image or copy the color code.
