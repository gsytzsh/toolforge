About Odd Even Lines

This tool splits text into odd-numbered lines and even-numbered lines. Line 1 is the first line.

It is useful for separating key-value pairs, alternating data, or extracting every other line from logs and exports.

How to Use Odd Even Lines

  1. Step 1: Paste your text into the input area.
  2. Step 2: Select the transformation you need (case change, remove lines, etc.).
  3. Step 3: Click the action button to apply the changes.
  4. Step 4: Copy the processed text for your use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a text length limit?
