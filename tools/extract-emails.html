About Extract Emails

This email extractor scans pasted text and lists the addresses it finds in a clean, deduplicated output. It is useful for processing logs, support exports, scraped text and unstructured notes.

You can paste large text blocks, quickly review the matches and copy only the email addresses you need for cleanup or further analysis.

How to Use Extract Emails

  1. Step 1: Paste your text into the input area.
  2. Step 2: Select the transformation you need (case change, remove lines, etc.).
  3. Step 3: Click the action button to apply the changes.
  4. Step 4: Copy the processed text for your use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a text length limit?
Most browsers handle text up to 100,000 characters well. Very large texts may cause slower performance.
Does this preserve formatting?
