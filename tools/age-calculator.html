About Age Calculator

This age calculator works out a person's age from a date of birth in years, months and days using the current date.

It is useful for forms, eligibility checks, school or insurance paperwork and any case where you need an exact age without counting manually.

How to Use Age Calculator

  1. Step 1: enter birth date and target date if needed
  2. Step 2: Confirm date format and timezone context for accurate day counts.
  3. Step 3: Calculate age in years, months and days.
  4. Step 4: Use output in forms, planning tools or eligibility checks.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Age Calculator mainly used for?
Age Calculator is mainly used to calculate exact age and date differences for planning and records.
Does Age Calculator send my input to a server?
For normal usage, Age Calculator processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
Why can age differ by one day across systems?
Timezone cutoffs and date parsing rules can shift boundary calculations. Keep date format and timezone consistent.
