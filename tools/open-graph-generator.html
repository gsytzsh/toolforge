About Open Graph Generator

This Open Graph generator creates OG meta tags for pages that need richer previews on Facebook, LinkedIn and other social platforms.

It is useful when shared links are missing the right title, description or image and you want social posts to look cleaner and more clickable.

How to Use Open Graph Generator

  1. Step 1: fill in og:title, og:description, og:url and image fields
  2. Step 2: Select the correct content type and image URL.
  3. Step 3: Generate Open Graph tags and review markup.
  4. Step 4: Copy tags into your page head and validate with social debuggers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Open Graph Generator mainly used for?
Open Graph Generator is mainly used to generate Open Graph tags for better social sharing previews.
Does Open Graph Generator send my input to a server?
For normal usage, Open Graph Generator processes data locally in your browser so you can test quickly without uploading project files.
Why is my shared image not updating on social platforms?
Platforms cache OG data. Re-scrape the URL in the platform debugger after updating your tags.
