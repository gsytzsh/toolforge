About Text Statistics & Word Counter

The Text Statistics & Word Counter tool lets you count words, characters and lines in one place for writing, SEO and content analysis.

It is useful for editing copy, cleaning pasted text, comparing content and making small text transformations directly in the browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are words counted?
Words are counted by splitting on whitespace. Hyphenated words count as one. Numbers and symbols with spaces count separately.
What is the difference between character counts?
Characters with spaces includes all characters. Characters without spaces excludes whitespace. UTF-8 bytes shows storage size.
How is reading time calculated?
Average reading speed is ~200-250 words per minute. The tool divides word count by 200 to estimate minutes needed.
Why does byte count differ from character count?
UTF-8 uses 1-4 bytes per character. ASCII characters use 1 byte, but emoji and special Unicode characters use more.
How are lines counted?
Lines are counted by newline characters. A single paragraph without breaks is 1 line. Each line break increments the count.
Is this accurate for non-English text?
Word counting varies by language. Chinese and Japanese don't use spaces, so word counts may be less accurate for those languages.
